# Phantom Engaged Pitch — Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Dark, authoritative, and data-driven. The aesthetic of a premium SaaS explainer — clean typography on deep navy, with teal accents matching the Phantom Engaged brand. Minimal decoration; the content carries weight. Motion is measured and deliberate, never flashy. Think: a senior consultant's keynote, not a startup hype reel.

## Colors

| Role       | Hex       | Usage                              |
| ---------- | --------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Background | `#1C2A3A` | Deep navy, all scenes              |
| Foreground | `#E8ECF1` | Primary text, high-contrast        |
| Accent     | `#2BA5A5` | Teal — highlights, key terms, CTA  |
| Muted      | `#8A9BB5` | Secondary text, labels, dividers   |
| Danger     | `#EF4444` | Risk callouts, warning signals     |

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Space Grotesk, 700-800 weight
- **Body:** Inter, 400 weight
- **Data/Labels:** JetBrains Mono, 400 weight

## Motion

- Entrance eases: `power3.out`, `expo.out`, `back.out(1.2)`
- Durations: 0.4–0.7s entrances, 0.3s transitions
- Stagger: 0.08–0.15s between elements
- Ambient: slow breathing glow on accent decoratives

## What NOT to Do

- No gradient text or neon accents
- No bouncy/elastic motion — this is serious content
- No pure white `#FFFFFF` backgrounds — always tinted
- No stock-photo imagery or placeholder icons
- No more than 3 colors visible per scene
